Before you get started on this form

It will take most people less than two minutes to complete this form. You will not need to provide any sensitive information about your child, but you may be asked if your child:

  • Is enrolled in programs like SNAP
  • Has been approved for free or reduced-price school meals
  • Has a household income that is eligible to receive Summer EBT

This form does not save your answers, and your answers will not be shared with anyone.
